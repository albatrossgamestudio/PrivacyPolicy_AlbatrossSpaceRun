Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how Albatross Space Run handles information for users of all ages, including children under 13, in accordance with Google Play policies and applicable privacy regulations.

Albatross Space Run does not collect, store, or share any personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or location data.

This game is designed to be suitable for all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect or solicit any personal data from children. If you believe that we have unintentionally collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Albatross Space Run uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect certain non-personal information automatically, such as:

  • Device type
  • Anonymous advertising identifiers (like AAID)
  • General app interaction data (such as ad views or taps)
This data is used to serve contextually appropriate ads. No personal data is shared with us as developers.

Learn more about how Google uses data: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

4. Data Security

We do not collect or transmit any personal or sensitive information. The app runs entirely on-device except for ad services provided by Google AdMob, which are securely handled by Google.
