Privacy Policy - Space Run

Effective Date: September 6, 2025

Developer: Albatross Game Studios

This privacy policy explains how Space Run ("we," "our," or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile game, in compliance with Google Play Store policies, COPPA, GDPR, and other applicable privacy laws.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Space Run does not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal information including:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Photos, videos, or device files
  • Contact lists or SMS data
  • Financial or payment information

The game functions entirely offline except for advertising services.

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Space Run is designed as a family-friendly game suitable for all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children of any age. Our game complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have inadvertently collected information from your child, please contact us immediately at albatrossgamestudio@gmail.com and we will delete such information promptly.

3. Google AdMob Integration

Space Run uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect certain device information for advertising purposes, including:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Anonymous advertising identifiers (Android Advertising ID)
  • App interaction data (ad views, clicks)
  • General location (country/region level only)

Important: This information is collected directly by Google AdMob, not by us. We do not have access to or control over this data collection.

For more information about Google's data practices, visit:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps

4. Data Security and Protection

Since Space Run does not collect personal data, we do not store or transmit any sensitive information. The app operates locally on your device. All advertising-related data handling is managed securely by Google AdMob using industry-standard encryption and security measures.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Space Run does not retain any personal data because we do not collect it. Game progress and high scores are stored locally on your device only. You can clear this data anytime by uninstalling the app.

For data collected by Google AdMob, please refer to Google's retention policies linked above.

6. Your Rights and Controls

You can control advertising personalization by:

  • Visiting your device's advertising settings
  • Using Google's Ad Settings at adssettings.google.com
  • Opting out of personalized ads in your device settings

7. Third-Party Services

Apart from Google AdMob, Space Run does not integrate with any other third-party services or share data with external parties.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" above. Continued use of Space Run after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
